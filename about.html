about us

Epic journeys

The world is filled with so much beauty that deserves to be seen. And travelers deserve better services when it comes to securing flights, booking accommodations, and searching for ideal adventures. Whether you want to experience the allure of the East and the draw of the West, you can trust that we here at Himalayas Travels offer the kind of trip you have in mind.

WHAT WE DO

Himalayas Travels provides wide-ranging travel-related services, from finding flights to looking for sightseeing tours. We over a variety of choices when it comes to airlines, hotels, car hire, and holiday packages. Here at Himalayas Travels, we understand how important it is to get the best travel deals, and we want to make sure you get exactly what you're looking for!

Travel has made the world more accessible, and we want nothing more than to see you have the best time while on holiday. With Himalayas Travels, you can find various travel packages, including tailored options, that meet your budget and needs. Whether you enjoy seeing cultural attractions or delight in taking city breaks, you will find ideal travel itineraries right here.

OUR TEAM

Making travel arrangements can get tricky, but we take pride in smooth flowing operations thanks to our patient, qualified, and very understanding staff. We understand the complexities of having to book flights, find hotels, and search for things to do while on vacation. Our very accommodating staff are on hand to help you with any concerns you have while planning your holidays.

WHAT WE OFFER

Here at Himalayas Travels, we cater to the diverse interests of our audience. We work with a number of airline partners that provide services that align with our clients. The travel destinations we offer our clients are frequently reviewed to ensure that safety and security of our clients comes first.

Our hotel affiliates cover a broad spectrum to ensure that there is a choice for every kind of budget. We also provide tailored tour packages for those seeking a specific kind of adventure, be it cultural in nature or centered on cuisine.
